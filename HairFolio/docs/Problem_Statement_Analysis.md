# Problem Statement & Analysis

**Background:**  
Small barbershops often rely on manual tracking of customer transactions, expenses, and income. This makes it hard to understand business performance, track trends, and plan for growth.

## Problems Identified
1. Manual record-keeping → errors and lost data
2. Limited visibility into performance → guesswork decision-making
3. Difficulty tracking expenses → inaccurate profit calculation
4. Trend & growth analysis → time-consuming manual calculations
5. Reporting limitations → slow, error-prone, non-scalable

## Consequences
- Reduced profitability
- Missed opportunities for improving services
- Difficulty planning for growth
